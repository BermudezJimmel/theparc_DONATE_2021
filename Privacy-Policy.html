Privacy Policy

The Performing Arts and Recreation Center (PARC) Foundation fully recognizes the importance of privacy and has implemented a range of security measures to protect your data. The PARC Foundation is completely committed to safeguarding your personal information against loss, illegal access, modification, or disclosure.

If you choose to use our service, you are granting The PARC Foundation permission to collect and use your personal information for the purposes of updating our records, providing up-to-date information about our work, and presenting more opportunities to support our efforts.

The data we process on you may include, but not limited to, your name, age, contact number, and email address, which can be used to keep you up to date on our latest information, respond to inquiries or suggestions, confirm any donation, submission, or registration made through the donation page, or collect payment for purchases made through the donation page.

You can cancel or bring your concerns at any time by calling 8350-6356 from Monday to Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM.
